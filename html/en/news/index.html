Announcements

The Saint Panteleimon Monastery on Mount Athos invites men who wish to work in the vineyards and agricultural grounds of the monastery on the Holy Mountain.

The brethren of the Saint Panteleimon Monastery provide transport, accommodation and meals in sketes and cells in the immediate vicinity of the place of work. The minimum stay is 1 calendar month; extension of the stay is possible by agreement with the abbot of the monastery, Archimandrite Evlogij.

Those wishing to go to Athos are asked to contact the rector of the church, priest Fr. Anatoli. Referral to the Saint Panteleimon Monastery takes place after an interview with Fr. Anatoli.
